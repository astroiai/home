News

Latest updates from the astronomy group

April 2026 Publication

Featured publication in The Astrophysical Journal

Our team has published a new study on star formation in early-universe galaxies, using data from the ALMA telescope and the VLT. The work was led by group researchers and represents a significant advance in understanding how the first massive stars formed.

March 2026 Personnel

New postdoctoral researcher joins the group

We welcome our new postdoc who strengthens our observational cosmology line. With experience in galaxy survey analysis, they will contribute to our ongoing projects with the Víctor M. Blanco Telescope at CTIO.

February 2026 Outreach

Public talk: The expanding universe

We held a public outreach event open to the Arica community at the UTA Velásquez Campus. More than 200 people attended the talk on the accelerating expansion of the universe and dark energy.

January 2026 Project

Fondecyt project approved

The group received funding from ANID's Fondecyt Regular program for the project "Galaxies at cosmic dawn: physical properties and evolution". The project runs for three years and will fund two postdoctoral positions.
